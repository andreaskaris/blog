ARP and the neighbor table

Document of reference: http://haifux.org/lectures/180/netLec2.pdf

Disabling ARP

NUD NOARP

From http://haifux.org/lectures/180/netLec2.pdf 

1
2
3
4
5
6
Neighboring Subsystem – states
● Special states:
● NUD_NOARP
● NUD_PERMANENT
● No state transitions are allowed from these states to another 
state.

This means that if an ARP entry ends up in NUD_NOARP, it will not time out, update or change in any way. An administrator needs to delete the ARP entry for ARP resolution to work again properly for the IP.
